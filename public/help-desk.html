Common Issues

How long does it take to process a report?
Report processing typically takes 24-48 hours. Priority issues may be handled sooner.
What information should I include in my report?
Include clear photos, location details, and a brief description of the infrastructure issue.
How can I check my report status?
You can check your report status in the "My Reports" section of your profile.
