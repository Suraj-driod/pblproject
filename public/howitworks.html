How Jansuvidha Works

Learn how our platform helps connect citizens with local authorities to resolve infrastructure issues efficiently.

1. Report an Issue

Take a photo of the infrastructure issue and submit it through our platform with location details.

2. Verification

Our team verifies the report and assigns it to the appropriate department for resolution.

3. Resolution

The assigned department takes action to resolve the reported issue.

4. Completion

Once resolved, you'll receive an update and can verify the solution.
